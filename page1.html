Interactive 3D Planning

Due to a rise in online shopping, malls and retailers across the United States  are set to decline in the coming years. As a result, mixed-use developments are being constructed in their place. These developments often include housing, resturants, entertainment, and more.
Built in 1980, Collin Creek Mall in Plano, Texas is a prime example of a slowing mall in desperate need of redevelopment. I wanted to design a redevelopment in 3D space so people could visualize the proposed development on any device.
